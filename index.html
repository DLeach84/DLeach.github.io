About me

I'm a Software Engineering student at Media Design School in Auckland, New Zealand. The focus of my study is Artifical Intelligence, but I'm learning all about Software Engineering as a discipline in the process.

I have been in the New Zealand Police for more than 17 years, and am currently working part time as a Detective while I complete my studies. After a number of years in the studies, it became time to move on to something new. I find the field of AI fascinating, and have lately been enjoying using AI to create art.
